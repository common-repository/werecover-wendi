== Description ==

For providers part of the WeRecover platform, streamline your inquiry engagement through our automated chat widget. Gather relevant info from potential inquiries and provide instant feedback on eligibility.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Who needs this plugin? =

If you have a WeRecover provider account and wish to utilize our automated inquiry management on your wordpress site.
